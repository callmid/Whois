# About Us
Welcome to /callmid! We're a compact local team of developers who live and breathe code, and believe that good software starts with understanding the people it's built for.

We don't chase trends for the sake of it, but we genuinely love technology and constantly expand our toolkit. We enjoy taking complex business logic, wrapping it in clean architecture, and delivering results we're proud to show.

# What We Do
- Backend Development: We build the server side for websites and Telegram bots. We design architecture, build APIs, work with databases, and configure server infrastructure. Our core stack is Python and Node.js, but we bring in other technologies when the task demands it.

- Frontend Development: We turn designs into fast, responsive, and user-friendly websites. Details matter — we craft with pixel-perfect precision and attention to micro-interactions.

- Regardless of scale, we focus on stability, speed, and transparency at every stage.

# Why Work With Us?
- Local Presence. We're right here, in your region. No time zone gaps, no getting lost in endless chats — we can meet in person, discuss your project over coffee, and come by when needed.

- Deep Engagement. We're not just executors — we're partners. We dive into your business model, ask the right questions, and propose solutions that work for your goals, not just the technical spec.

- Flexible Approach. Whether it's a one-off task, a long-term collaboration, or strengthening an existing team — we adapt to the format that suits you best.

- Human Communication. We speak plain language, free of technical snobbery. We keep you in the loop on progress, explain complex matters clearly, and are always open to feedback.

# Let's Discuss Your Project
We're always excited about new challenges and interesting ideas. If you need a website from scratch, a Telegram bot for your business or automation, or just want to talk things through — reach out. We'll gladly listen, offer options, and chances are, we can help.
